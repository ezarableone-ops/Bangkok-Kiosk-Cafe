Our Menu

Street Flavours, Perfected

Every dish is cooked to order, just like the legendary pushcarts of Bangkok's Yaowarat Road.

Classic

Pad Thai

ผัดไทย

Silky rice noodles tossed in a tangy-sweet tamarind sauce with crunchy bean sprouts, crushed peanuts, and a squeeze of fresh lime. Thailand's most iconic street dish — done right.

Pork Chicken Shrimp Squid
Authentic

Kao Kook Kapi

ข้าวคลุกกะปิ

Fragrant rice stir-fried with aromatic shrimp paste, served with caramelised sweet pork, crispy dried shrimp, a jammy omelette, and a rainbow of fresh condiments. Deep, bold, and unforgettable.

Comfort

Kao Mun Kai

ข้าวมันไก่

Silky poached chicken resting on ginger-infused jasmine rice, drizzled with a garlicky soybean sauce and served with a fiery chilli-ginger dip. Bangkok's favourite comfort food.

Wok-Fired

Pad Si Ew

ผัดซีอิ๊ว

Wide flat noodles kissed by a scorching-hot wok, glazed in dark sweet soy, tossed with tender Chinese broccoli and egg. Smoky, savoury, and wildly addictive.

Vegetarian

Tofu Spicy

เต้าหู้ผัดพริก

Crispy golden tofu bathed in a fiery chilli-basil sauce with fresh vegetables and aromatic herbs. Packed with heat and flavour — proof that plant-based can punch hard.

★ Popular

Kao Pad Kaprao

ข้าวผัดกระเพรา

Thailand's undisputed #1 street meal. Minced pork stir-fried with fiery bird's-eye chillies and holy basil, crowned with a crispy fried egg over steaming jasmine rice. Simple. Perfect. Legendary.
